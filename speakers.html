Our Keynote Speakers

Mr. Rajesh Laskary (CISSP, CISM, CEH, COBIT, ISO, PMP)

Information Security Manager, Singapore
  • B.E.
  • PGDBM

Mr. Rajesh Laskary is a cybersecurity professional & public speaker currently working as an Information Security Manager in Singapore. Offering 15+ years of rich experience in leading end to end life cycles of complex Information & Cyber Security related projects in Cloud Security, Application Security- SAST, DAST etc, Technology Risk Management, Security Governance, Risk, Compliance & Audit, IAM (Identity & Access Management), LAM (Logical Access Management), Security Control Implementation, Credit Cards(PCI DSS), Payments, Insurance, and Healthcare (HIPPA). Has also worked in Cyber Security Controls Centre (CSCC) of GIS(Global Information Security), Barclays Capital Singapore.Experience in working on large projects involving PCI-DSS, HIPPA, ERISA, COBIT, SOX compliance, Third party Credit Scoring & Rating agencies.

Dr. Devesh Kumar Srivastava

Professor, Department of Information Technology SCIT, Manipal University Jaipur
  • MACM
  • MIAENG

Dr. Devesh Kumar Srivastava has been working as a professor in the School of Computing and Information Technology at Manipal University Jaipur. He has been associated with this university since August 2012. He has rich and varied job experience of more than 18 years, including teaching, software development, research and administration in top academic institutes and multinational organization. He has been coordinator for various departmental and university level activities like academic, registration, admission and discipline. His research area are Software Engineering, Data Mining and Cloud Computing. Under his supervision one research scholar has been awarded PhD degree in cloud computing from Uttrakhand Technical University Dehradun and another one is awarded PhD degree in Image Processing from Manipal University Jaipur. Four scholars have been pursuing their PhD under his supervision. He has guided eight M.Tech and ten B.Tech students for their final year major projects and some are pursuing. He is professional member of ACM and have been a faculty sponsor of ACM student chapter during 2015-16. He has published total 53 research papers in peer reviewed referred Journals and research papers through conferences of IEEE/ Springer/ ACM and Elsevier papers and still few papers have been accepted for publishing. He chaired 17 technical sessions and addressed 6 invited talks in the various international conferences. He has reviewed conference papers and journal papers. He has been honoured with awards for the year 2017 and 2018 for the big contribution in the research work. He has been organising member, program committee member, advisory member of several conferences and organized expert talks and workshops in university premises.
